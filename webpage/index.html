Cindy: When should I go to the airport?

The second client is Cindy who lives in North Druid Hills. She needs to figure out when to travel to the airport and get back to decide on her air tickets.

This map shows where Cindy lives and where Cindy wants to travel to and from. Two sites are converted in the route between her home address and the airport, site 089-3323 in North Druid Hills, and site 121-5468 around the airport. The tow sites are selected on the map.

This visualization shows the average speed by hour of each site. The color encodes the value of the Speed Index computed with the following equation.

\( \frac{\text{Current Speed} - \text{Speed Limit}}{\text{Speed Limit}} \)

If Speed Index <0, it’s red, meaning the traffic is slower than the speed limit. If Speed Index >0, it’s green, meaning the traffic is faster than the speed limit.

In general, the traffic situation around her home at site 089-3323 is better than that around the airport at site 121-5468.

This view shows the average traffic speed of the site 089-3323 in both directions. Northbound is traveling from the airport back home, while Southbound is traveling from home to the airport.

According to the chart, driving along Northbound (from the airport back home) during 15:00 -- 19:00 is slower than the other time. On the other hand, driving along Southbound (from home to the airport) during 7:00 -- 9:00 is slower than other time

This view shows the average traffic speed of the site 121-5468 in both directions. Northbound is traveling from home to the airport, while Southbound is traveling from the airport back to home.

According to the chart, driving along Northbound (from the airpot to home) from 7:00-17:00 is slower than the other time. On the other hand, driving along Southbound (from home to the airport) duirng 17:00 -- 1:00 is slower than other time

Now the result seems to be clear! She needs to avoid 7:00 -- 9:00 and 17:00 -- 1:00 when driving to the airport, and 7:00-17:00 and 15:00 -- 19:00 when driving from the airport back home!

Therefore, if just considering traffic situation between home and the airport, she decided to travel to the airport between 10:00 and 16:00 and travel back after 20:00.
